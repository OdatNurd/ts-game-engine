About Collision-Test

This is test code for checking out new collision code in ts-game-engine

The scene is made up of a series of collision shapes with their origins at various locations. A collision shape (or point) follows the mouse, and collision checks are done based on that shape/point.

When a collision is detected, the shape renders as red to indicate this.

Additionally, two line segments are displayed, which can be moved around by dragging their endpoints. The intersection point of the two lines is visualized, assuming that there is one and that it falls on the stage.

Line intersection of the lines renders in one of two colors; If the intersection of the two lines falls where both segments physically intersect, the dot is green.

If instead the intersection falls on the projection of one or both lines, the dot is red.

This allows for simultaneous testing of line segment as well as line intersections.

Collision Test Controls

P
Change collision type to 'POINT'
R
Change collision type to 'RECTANGLE'
C
Change collision type to 'CIRCLE'
1-5
In CIRCLE/RECTANGLE mode, change the origin point of the collision shape
Mouse
Control collision shape/point; drag line points to show line intersections
